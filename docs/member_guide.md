# Table Reservation System — Member Guide

Welcome to the **Member Guide**! Creating an account unlocks exclusive perks, reservation history, and priority rules.

## 1. Registering an Account

- **Sign Up**: You can create an account using your Name, Email, and Password on the public customer portal.
- **Profile Management**: Once registered, you have a unified `customerId` across all participating restaurants on the platform.

## 2. Common Members

As a standard logged-in user:
- **Reservation History**: You have access to a personalized dashboard where you can view all your upcoming and past reservations across any restaurant using the system.
- **Faster Checkout**: Your contact details autofill during the booking process.
- **Protection**: Unlike Unauthenticated Guests, your reservations are tied to your Member Account, providing a layer of protection against standard priority overrides.

## 3. Premium Members (VIP)

The **Premium** tier is our highest membership level, providing unmatched access to our most popular venues.
- **Priority Overbooking Power**: Premium members have an exclusive "cut the line" privilege.
- **How it Works**: If you attempt to book a highly sought-after time slot and the only available table is currently held by an unauthenticated Guest, the system's **Conflict Resolution Engine** will automatically step in.
- **The Override**: The system will cancel the Guest's unauthenticated reservation and award the table to you instantly. This ensures our Premium Members always get the best experience possible. 

*Note: You cannot override a reservation held by another registered Member.*
